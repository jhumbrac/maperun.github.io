A glimpse into the life of me

My Story

Ever since I was a teenager, Ive had a passion for traveling. The thought of entering the work force right after college didnt appeal to me, so I tried to figure out a way to travel and earn an income at the same time. Fortunately, modeling provided me with this opportunity. Over the last 15 years, Ive had the privilege of traveling and modeling in numerous countries across the globe, working for some of the most prominent designers in the fashion industry. Along the way, I cultivated relationships with some of the most amazing people, whom I now consider friends. I can honestly say that traveling and modeling was one the most rewarding experiences of my life.
